About Rajasthan

Rajasthan, located in the northwestern part of India, is a land of royal palaces, ancient forts, vibrant culture, and the vast Thar Desert. Known for its rich history, Rajasthan is a dream destination for history lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and culture seekers.

Popular Tourist Spots

Amber Fort

Explore the magnificent Amber Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage site with stunning architecture and panoramic views of the surrounding hills.

City Palace, Jaipur

Visit the opulent City Palace in Jaipur, a beautiful blend of Mughal and Rajasthani architecture, offering a glimpse into royal life.

Mehrangarh Fort

Discover the grandeur of Mehrangarh Fort in Jodhpur, one of the largest forts in India, offering breathtaking views of the blue city below.
