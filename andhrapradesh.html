About Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh, located on the southeastern coast of India, is a land rich in culture, history, and natural beauty. Known for its historical temples, beautiful beaches, and lush landscapes, it offers a mix of both spiritual and scenic experiences.

Popular Tourist Spots

Tirumala Venkateswara Temple

Visit the world-famous Tirumala Venkateswara Temple in Tirupati, one of the holiest shrines for Hindus and an architectural marvel.

Araku Valley

Explore the scenic beauty of Araku Valley, a lush green hill station surrounded by coffee plantations and picturesque landscapes.

Rishikonda Beach

Relax at Rishikonda Beach in Visakhapatnam, known for its golden sands and clear waters, perfect for a peaceful retreat or water sports.
