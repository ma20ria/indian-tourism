About Jammu and Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir, nestled in the northernmost part of India, is renowned for its snow-capped mountains, beautiful valleys, and enchanting gardens. Known as the "Paradise on Earth," it is a perfect destination for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and peace seekers.

Popular Tourist Spots

Dal Lake

Experience the iconic Dal Lake, known for its Shikaras (traditional wooden boats) and picturesque surroundings in Srinagar.

Gulmarg

Visit Gulmarg, a paradise for winter sports lovers, offering breathtaking views and thrilling skiing experiences.

Pahalgam

Discover the beauty of Pahalgam, a serene valley surrounded by lush greenery, pristine rivers, and majestic mountains.
